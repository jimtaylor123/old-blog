How to make doughnuts from stale bread - breadnuts

 February 1, 2020 | Image: Luciene Taylor

Here's a great recipe using old bread my wife came up with the other day. Apparently these are called "rabanadas" in Argentina and Brazil. I call them breadnuts (i.e. doughnuts made from bread).

1. Mix eggs and milk, bit of sugar

2. Dip slices of stale bread in egg and milk mix

3. Shallow fry until golden brown

4. Dip in sugar and cinammon

These are a delicious way to use up old bread. Stale bread is even better. Apparently you can also do the above with a jam sandwich to make a kind of jam doughnut.
